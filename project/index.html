Welcome to Forage Focus— the website of the Leighton Buzzard Foraging Club!

We are a group of people who enjoy finding and using wild plants from the environment in and around Leighton Buzzard. We like to share tips about the best places to forage, recipes and other uses for the things we find, and we meet up regularly in the local pub to discuss the hobby. (Alas, because of the Covid regulations, the meetups have currently been suspended).
